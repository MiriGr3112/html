The Corona Virus and The Day After

If we deal with current affairs or the life we live in right now, we will not be able to ignore the virus that has erupted on our planet. The beginning of the epidemic seemed half a joke or half a movie, in which every person on earth had a hard time believe what dimensions it would become. The days went by, and day by day the whole thing became more serious, more real, and everyone looked at it from a different perspective. So how did it all start? Let's see the corona message to us.


The effects of the disease are many, on all areas of life, for example:

  • Health
  • Economy
  • Education
  • Career
  • Politics
  • Environment
  • Social closeness

After each and every one of us lives have changed, we need to hold on to a wish - that one day it will be behind us, that we will learn to live with it as with the rest of the existing viruses, that we can figure points from this period for our personal and mass improvement, learn and appreciate the simple and basic things in life. So let's talk about the day after...

Back to Life - Back to Reality

Once this difficult period is over, we will return to the days when we are:


We will take off masks
We return to our job and team
Everyone can go back and get an education
We will create a new opportunities for our future
We will create new families on earth
We will be able to travel long distances and travel the world
We can celebrate: at events, concerts, clubs and festivals
We can return doing sports and also watching it
We will be able to enjoy culinary experiences in restaurants and cafes
We can be able to do shooping at the mall or a market
We can enjoy nature after rehabilitation
We will return to physical interaction, meet with friends and families, talk through screens and hug each other


We are nearing the end, take care of each other. Even the day after.
